About Us

Feeding your families for less, every day!

Food for Less is a Social Impact Enterprise founded and run by Kenyans, which tackles inefficiencies in the food system and achieve zero hunger. The enterprise is committed to providing access to affordable and nutritious food to everyone, without any profit motive. We work with local farmers and distribution partners to source produce that would otherwise be wasted. While food prices continue to soar, 60% of our communities struggle with food insecurity, despite 30% of food being wasted during the harvest and distribution processes. Zero hunger is critical to building a better future for everyone. Achieving this will positively impact our economies, health, education, equality, environment, and social development. Food for Less invites everyone to join their revolutionary food sourcing movement to change the future for the better, one order at a time.
