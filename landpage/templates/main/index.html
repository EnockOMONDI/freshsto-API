Why Food For Less?

Subsidized prices

Our partners supply highly nutritious fruits and vegetables at a lower cost through Food for Less. These items may be of a lower grade or near expiry, but we strive to provide them at a highly subsidized price to help you save more and pay less.

Convenience

Save precious time. Order with just a click of a button and enjoy hassle-free delivery to the nearest vendor in your neighborhood.

Creating sustainable communities and business opportunities

At Food for Less, we work with local farmers to source produce that may have been wasted otherwise. By doing so, we help increase their income while also supporting a network of suppliers, delivery agents, and vendors who earn a living whilst feeding their community.

Fighting hunger, Reducing waste and Saving our environment

Food for Less orders combat both food waste and hunger with 30% of food wasted during harvest and distribution and 60% of communities experiencing food insecurity. Ordering will also reduce negative impacts on finances and the environment caused by wasted resources.
